What is Ignite Shreveport - Bossier

Ignite Shreveport - Bossier is a night of rapid-fire talks that are meant to do 3 things - Enlighten you [about a topic], Inspire you [to take action], and Delight you [in an unexpected way]. Ignite talks are a chance for members of the community to "geek out" about topics that they are passionate about.

You'll be hearing from 12 speakers from the community. Each speaker has 20 auto-advancing slides(15 seconds per slide) to share a passion / topic of their's for 5 minutes.


You'll hear about topics ranging from Startups to Art to Helping Others to Movies and everything in between.

Ignite Shreveport - Bossier aims to rally the Shreveport - Bossier community around interesting ideas and to show that interesting ideas can come from anyone.

Tickets

Ignite Shreveport - Bossier is FREE and open to the public. No tickets required! We have a local "charity of the night" that you can make donations to in person on the night of Ignite. We will also have a "Pandabox" (from Pandabit.com) available on the night of Ignite that you can put your old / used electronics in. The money raised from the "Pandabox" will also go to our "charity of the night".

For this event, our charity is the Foodbank of Northwest Louisiana.
